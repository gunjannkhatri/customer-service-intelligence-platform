# Business Context

## Company: NovaCare Solutions

NovaCare Solutions is a B2B customer support outsourcing company
managing 500 agents across 6 departments:

- Inbound Call Support
- Technical Helpdesk
- Billing & Accounts
- Enterprise Client Support
- Chat & Email Support
- Quality Assurance

## The Problem

### Problem 1 — Fragmented Data
Agent performance data lived in three systems:
- Call System → call volume, handle time, CSAT
- Ticket System → ticket count, resolution time, SLA status
- HR System (SQL Server) → employee info, attendance, shifts

No single view existed. Managers made decisions based on
incomplete information.

### Problem 2 — Reactive Management
SLA breaches were only discovered during weekly review meetings.
By then, the damage was already done. Clients had already
escalated complaints.

### Problem 3 — Manual Reporting
Every Monday, 3 analysts spent 4–6 hours each pulling data
from different systems into Excel. The report was sent by
Wednesday — already 2 days stale.

### Problem 4 — Unfair Scoring
Each team lead had their own definition of "good performance."
Agents in different departments were measured differently.
High performers felt unrecognized. Low performers had no
clear targets to improve against.

## The Solution

A unified Customer Service Intelligence Platform that:

1. Pulls all data automatically every morning via ADF
2. Applies consistent KPI definitions across all departments
3. Computes a transparent 0–100 Agent Performance Score
4. Shows live rankings on office TV screens
5. Sends alerts before SLA breaches escalate
6. Delivers PDF reports automatically — zero manual effort
